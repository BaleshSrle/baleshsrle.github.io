Godina je 1979. i u jednoj njujorškoj bolnici na Menhetnu se rodila djevojčica. Istog dana, mladi sveštenik na nebu primjećuje lučnu putanju komete iznad mjeseca (što je u filmu opisano kao "Božije Oko"), i o toj pojavi obavještava Papu. Katolička crkva već vjekovima strahuje od ovog događaja, a njihovi strahovi se potvrđuju kada se otkrije da novorođena djevojčica imenom Kristina, na tijelu nosi obilježje Antihrista The year is 1979 and a girl was born in a New York hospital in Manhattan. That same day, a young priest in the sky notices an arcing path of a comet above the moon (described in the movie as the "Eye of God"), and informs the Pope about the phenomenon. The Catholic Church has been afraid of this event for centuries, and their fears are confirmed when it is discovered that a newborn girl named Cristine bears the mark of the Antichrist on her body.

Jericho Kane, a suicidal ex-cop and alcoholic, depressed after the contract killing of his wife and daughter, works for a private security company and blames God for his suffering. Together with his partner Bobby Chicago, he works as security for a banker, whose body is possessed by Satan.
